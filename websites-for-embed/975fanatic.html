97.5 The Fanatic: Philadelphia's First FM Sports Station

97.5 The Fanatic is outstanding sports coverage for fans passionate about local and national sports. Featuring big-time hosts -- Mike & Mike, Brian Baldinger, Tony Bruno & Harry Mayes, and Mike Missanelli -- 97.5 The Fanatic entertains and informs with great star interviews and passionate caller discussions. Stay up-to-date with all the latest news surrounding your favorite teams: Phillies, Eagles, Flyers, Sixers, and More. Get the latest contest and event info, and sign up to be an MVP here.
